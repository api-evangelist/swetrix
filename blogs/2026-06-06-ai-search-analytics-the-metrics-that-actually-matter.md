AI search tools change visitor behavior in ways traditional pageview metrics miss, requiring a new scorecard of key performance indicators. The article recommends tracking AI referral sessions, landing pages, assisted conversions, event completion, funnel drop-off, revenue by source, content freshness, and branded versus non-branded traffic to understand true AI search impact. Organizations should pair Swetrix's cookieless analytics platform with Google Search Console to measure the complete journey from AI referrer click through conversion and revenue.
