AI search traffic does not arrive through one clean path: a buyer can click a ChatGPT source, copy a Perplexity citation, search a brand after a Google AI Overview, or return via direct traffic. Swetrix provides cookieless analytics, referrers, UTM tracking, custom events, goals, funnels, user and session analysis, and revenue tracking to connect those paths to money without collecting personal data.
