Apple's Intelligent Tracking Prevention fractures cross-device user journeys and hides the original source of marketing conversions, causing analysts on default tracking setups to credit the search engine and ignore the social media ad that drove a sale. The article argues marketing teams must adopt privacy-first analytics to restore data accuracy without relying on fragile browser storage.
