The article explains that 8% of retail shoppers abandon more than 10 purchases per year due to broken or slow websites, and provides techniques including global error handlers, try/catch blocks, and source maps for catching bugs. Engineers can filter third-party noise and protect user privacy through anonymized telemetry rather than cookie-dependent tracking using Swetrix's cookieless analytics platform.
